# Copy Style Adaptation

Use this reference when writing usable profile copy. The goal is not to imitate a celebrity exactly or pretend the client is someone else. Use public-figure-like communication archetypes only as tone inspiration: sentence rhythm, emotional temperature, level of humor, and directness.

## Style Selection Process

1. Infer the client’s communication temperament from P/L/A/T/G/C fields.
2. Choose one primary writing archetype and one secondary support.
3. Write in the client's facts and boundaries.
4. Remove any sentence that sounds like a consultant, ad copy, or generic dating-app template.

Do not write “模仿名人 X”. Instead write “表达气质参考：克制温柔型 / 松弛幽默型 / 清醒理性型...”.

## Archetypes

### 克制温柔型

Use when the client is slow-warm, stable, thoughtful, gentle, and values long-term companionship.

Writing habits:

- Short, warm sentences.
- Specific life details.
- No exaggerated self-praise.
- Expectations are gentle but clear.

Example tone:

“我不是特别外放的人，但相处久了会比较稳定。平时生活不算热闹，做饭、运动、整理家，会让我觉得日子有在好好往前走。希望遇到一个沟通舒服、愿意认真了解彼此的人。”

### 松弛幽默型

Use when the client is lively, socially comfortable, playful, and wants an easygoing relationship.

Writing habits:

- Light jokes.
- Concrete scenes.
- Self-aware but not self-deprecating too much.
- Keep boundaries soft.

Example tone:

“工作日认真上班，周末认真恢复电量。会做饭，但也尊重外卖在人类文明里的位置。希望遇到一个聊得来、情绪稳定、愿意一起把普通日子过得有点意思的人。”

### 清醒理性型

Use when the client is structured, direct, career-aware, and values planning and boundaries.

Writing habits:

- Clear relationship intention.
- Concrete expectations.
- Warmth through responsibility, not甜腻.
- Avoid sounding like a job description.

Example tone:

“现在的生活和工作都比较稳定，也更清楚自己想要什么。比起短暂的新鲜感，我更看重沟通、边界和一起解决问题的能力。希望认识一个同样认真、现实感强、愿意把关系往前推进的人。”

### 明亮生活感型

Use when the client has visible hobbies, aesthetic taste, life energy, travel/food/home details, or expressive warmth.

Writing habits:

- More sensory detail.
- More everyday scenes.
- Optimistic but grounded.
- Let hobbies show personality.

Example tone:

“喜欢把生活过得具体一点：好好吃饭、规律运动，偶尔去看展或找一家喜欢的小店。关系里我会比较看重分享欲和回应感，希望两个人不只是合适，也能一起把日子过得有光。”

### 成熟照顾型

Use when the client’s strengths are responsibility, family awareness, care, patience, emotional stability.

Writing habits:

- Stable, reassuring wording.
- Show care through actions.
- Avoid “我很会照顾人” without examples.
- Expectations emphasize reciprocity.

Example tone:

“我在关系里比较看重稳定和互相照顾。不是那种很会说漂亮话的人，但会把在意落在具体事情上。希望遇到一个愿意沟通、也珍惜稳定关系的人，两个人一起把生活安排好。”

## Rewrite Checks

Before finalizing any copy:

- Does it sound like a real person could paste it into a dating profile?
- Does every trait have a concrete detail or scene?
- Is there at least one light conversation hook?
- Are expectations warm rather than demanding?
- Does it avoid generic phrases like “三观一致”“真诚善良”“希望遇见对的人” unless rewritten with detail?
